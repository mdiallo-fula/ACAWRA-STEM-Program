Overview

The STEM Program will resume on September 16 with the robotics section starting the 27th of the same month. In addition to the tutoring sessions, the program will also offer a recreational activity: basketball games. These games will be open to all high school students enrolled in the program. An indoor basketball court will be provided about twice a month. All educational activities will take place Monday through Friday, from 4 PM to 7 PM, at 675 Queen St S, Kitchener, ON N2M 1A1 , except robotics. Parents who register their children will receive the exact schedule and locations for all activities.

Courses

Math + Physics + Chemistry

  • Get help mastering concepts taught at school
  • Develop effective study strategies and exam preparation techniques
  • Gain advanced problem solving skills through contest preparation (math only)
  • Conduct science experiments for a hands-on exploration of topics taught at school

Web Development

  • Learn how to create responsive web pages with HTML and CSS
  • Add interactive functionalities to websites with JavaScript (grades 9-12 only)

Robotics

  • Introduction to mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering
  • Design a simple car via tinkercad.com and 3D print it
  • Install electronics on the car: motors, proximity sensors, ...
  • Program the car so that it can drive and avoid obstacles
