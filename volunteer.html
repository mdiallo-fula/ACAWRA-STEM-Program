Overview

All our programs have been made possible thanks to the dedication of numerous volunteers, and the STEM Program is no exception. We are seeking enthusiastic upper-year UW students (2A and above) in Math, Science, or Engineering who are passionate about teaching and enjoy working with children or teenagers (grades 7-12). If this sounds like you, please apply for the roles listed below; your help will be very appreciated. Tutoring will take place in-person at 675 Queen St S, Kitchener, ON N2M 1A1 between 5 PM and 8 PM. The expected weekly time commitment is approximately 2 hours.

Roles

Math/Physics Tutor

As a Math/Physics tutor, you'll help students master their schoolwork and develop advanced analytical skills through interactive problem-solving sessions. You'll guide them through challenging problems from cemc.uwaterloo.ca and their curriculum's textbooks, encouraging them to craft elegant solutions.
Note: you can choose to tutor Math only or Physics only.

Web Development Tutor

As a Web Development tutor, you'll introduce students to programming and web design, providing a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. You'll guide them in turning creative ideas into functioning websites and applications, using resources such as w3schools.com and freecodecamp.org.
