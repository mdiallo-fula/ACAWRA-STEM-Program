Overview

All our programs have been made possible thanks to the dedication of numerous volunteers, and the STEM Program is no exception. We are seeking enthusiastic students in Math, Science, or Engineering who are passionate about teaching and enjoy working with children or teenagers (grades 5-12). If this sounds like you, please apply for the roles listed below; your help will be very appreciated. Tutoring will take place in-person at 675 Queen St S, Kitchener, ON N2M 1A1 between 4 PM and 7 PM. The minimum weekly time commitment is 1 hour.

Roles

Math/Physics/Chemistry Tutor

You'll help students master their schoolwork and develop advanced analytical skills through interactive problem-solving sessions. You'll guide them through challenging problems from cemc.uwaterloo.ca and their curriculum's textbooks, encouraging them to write elegant solutions.
Note: you can choose to tutor only one subject.

Web Development Tutor

You'll introduce students to programming and web design, providing a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. You'll guide them in turning creative ideas into functioning websites and applications, using resources such as w3schools.com, freecodecamp.org, and code.org.
